Featured Holiday Drinks

Discover our handcrafted specialties — each cup brewed with care, warmth, and a touch of Café 4080’s signature flavor.

Cinnamon Whirl Latte

A swirl of comfort in every sip. The Cinnamon Whirl Latte blends freshly pulled espresso with steamed milk, a drizzle of wild honey, and a dash of hand-ground Ceylon cinnamon. Topped with a soft crown of foam dusted in spice, it’s warm, aromatic, and gently sweet — like your favorite blanket on a cold morning.

Ingredients: espresso, steamed milk, Ceylon cinnamon, wild honey, vanilla extract, foam.

Midnight Mocha Muse

For dreamers and late-night thinkers, this drink is your muse. The Midnight Mocha Muse combines rich dark chocolate with a double shot of espresso and a hint of hazelnut syrup. Finished with silky whipped cream and chocolate shavings, it’s the perfect harmony of bold and indulgent — deep enough to spark inspiration, smooth enough to savor slowly.

Ingredients: espresso, dark cocoa, hazelnut syrup, milk, whipped cream, chocolate shavings.

Gingerbread Serenade

Sweet, spiced, and unapologetically cozy — the Gingerbread Serenade is a seasonal favorite that deserves to stay all year. Crafted with our signature espresso, steamed milk, gingerbread spice syrup, and a sprinkle of nutmeg, it sings with notes of molasses and warmth. Each cup feels like a soft carol in a mug.

Ingredients: espresso, milk, gingerbread syrup, molasses, nutmeg, whipped topping.
